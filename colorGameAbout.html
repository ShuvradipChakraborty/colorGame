About The Great RGB Color Game!

Hello world! This version of 'The Great RGB Color Game' was developed by Weston Ludeke as part of the Udemy course, The Web Developer Bootcamp by Colt Steele".

The object of the game is to look at the three ramdomly generated numbers and determine which of the color squares represent the correct color. RGB is Red, Green, and Blue on the color model.
